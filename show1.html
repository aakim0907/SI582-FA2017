Sleep Therapy for Depression

We are testing 2 different forms of sleep therapy to help people with trouble falling asleep and depression. We will see how these therapies help sleep in people with depression.

$80
Ann Arbor, MI
About Eligibility Criteria
1~2 times a week

Behavioral study

You will be asked to keep a sleep diary for a week, and come in to talk with our researchers about your condition. You will be radomly assigned to one of two therapy groups. If you're assigned to the first group, you will only need to come in once to learn about various methods that will improve your sleep. If you're assigned to the second group, you will be required to come in two times to complete a sleep retaining session.

Principal Investigator: Ashley Fontanna

Study posted on: November 21st, 2017
