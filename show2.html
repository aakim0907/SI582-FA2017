Sleep Therapy for Depression

We are testing 2 different forms of sleep therapy to help people with trouble falling asleep and depression. We will see how these therapies help sleep in people with depression.

$80
Ann Arbor, MI
About Eligibility Criteria
This trial requires some eligibility criteria that is not covered in our Eligibility Check
  • Age (years) is between 18 and 65
  • Currently has Insomnia
  • Willing to not use any sleep mediciation during study
  • Currently taking antidepressant for at least 8 weeks OR not taking any antidepressants and you do not plan to start one in the next 9 weeks
